SportFree Privacy Policy Summary

At SportFree, we treat privacy as a strategic foundation, not a compliance afterthought. This summary provides a concise overview of how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. For full details, please review the complete SportFree Privacy Policy below.

How We Collect and Use Your Personal Information

To register and access SportFree services, you may be required to provide your account name, password, mobile phone number, or authenticate via third-party platforms (such as WeChat).

When you enable specific features (e.g., cycling route tracking), we may collect precise location data, device information, nickname, avatar, and content you voluntarily publish, strictly limited to clearly stated purposes.

During your use of SportFree, we also collect de-identified usage logs and device signals to ensure system stability, security risk control, and experience optimization. These data do not directly identify you.

For more details, please refer to the Personal Information Collection List.

Device Permissions We May Request

Certain SportFree features require access to device permissions, including location, camera, photo library, microphone, and contacts. All permissions follow a principle of necessity, transparency, and revocability.

You may review and manage permission status at any time in your device system settings. Details are available in the Permission Usage List.

How We Share Your Information

SportFree adheres to a strict “no unnecessary sharing” policy. We do not disclose your personal information to unrelated third parties unless required to deliver core services or comply with applicable laws.

Where collaboration is essential, we may share limited data with affiliated companies, technical service providers, analytics partners, and push service vendors, all governed by contractual and technical safeguards.

Please refer to the Third-Party Data Sharing and SDK List for details.

Your Rights and Controls

You retain full control over your personal information, including the rights to access, correct, delete, withdraw consent, and close your account. These options are available directly within the SportFree App.

If manual assistance is required, we will respond within 15 business days.

Protection of Minors

SportFree services are primarily designed for adults. If you are under 18, please use our services with parental or guardian consent.

For children under 14, we apply a dedicated Children’s Personal Information Protection Policy .

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or complaints regarding privacy or data protection, please contact us via the in-app Contact Us feature or email us at 258066526@qq.com. Identity verification may be required to ensure secure processing.
